## 2. The Clean Core Architecture Strategy

### Revolutionizing Customization Approaches

The "Clean Core" strategy represents a fundamental shift in how organizations approach ERP customization and extension. By moving customizations away from the core S/4HANA system and onto the **SAP Business Technology Platform (BTP)**, organizations can maintain system integrity while enabling innovation and agility.

**Core Benefits of Clean Core:**

- **Simplified Upgrades**: System updates and new feature adoption occur without risk to custom functionality.
- **Independent Scaling**: Extensions and customizations can scale based on specific business needs without affecting core system performance.
- **Accelerated Innovation**: Development teams can rapidly prototype and deploy new capabilities using modern development tools and methodologies.
- **Enhanced Security**: Side-by-side extensions benefit from enterprise-grade security without compromising core system integrity.

The side-by-side extension model enables organizations to build sophisticated custom applications that integrate seamlessly with S/4HANA while maintaining operational independence:

