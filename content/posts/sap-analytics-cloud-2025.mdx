## The Analytics Revolution in Enterprise

### From Descriptive to Prescriptive Analytics

Traditional analytics approaches focused primarily on descriptive analytics—understanding what happened in the past through reports and dashboards. Modern enterprises require the full spectrum of analytics capabilities: descriptive (what happened), diagnostic (why it happened), predictive (what will happen), and prescriptive (what should we do about it). SAP Analytics Cloud 2025 delivers this complete analytics lifecycle through integrated tools and intelligent automation.

### Democratization of Data and Analytics

The transformation from centralized IT-controlled reporting to self-service analytics has fundamentally changed how organizations use data. SAP Analytics Cloud 2025 accelerates this democratization by providing intuitive tools that enable business users to access, analyze, and visualize data without requiring deep technical expertise, while maintaining the governance and security required for enterprise-grade analytics.
