## The Evolution of Enterprise Platform Technology

### From Monolithic to Composable Architecture

Traditional enterprise platforms were built around monolithic architectures that required significant customization and complex integration efforts. Modern enterprises require composable architectures that enable rapid assembly of business capabilities from reusable components, services, and APIs. SAP BTP 2025 embodies this composable approach, providing a foundation for building flexible, scalable, and maintainable enterprise solutions.

### The Rise of Citizen Development

The democratization of application development through low-code and no-code platforms has transformed who can build enterprise applications. SAP BTP 2025 accelerates this trend by providing sophisticated yet accessible development tools that enable business users to create powerful applications while maintaining the enterprise-grade security, scalability, and governance required for mission-critical solutions.
