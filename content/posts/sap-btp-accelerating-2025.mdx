The evolution of enterprise development platforms reflects the changing needs of modern organizations:

**Traditional Development (2000s-2010s)**:

- Complex infrastructure management and maintenance

**Modern Platform Approach (2015-Present)**:

- Cloud-native architecture with managed services

**Intelligent Platform Era (2020s-Future)**:
